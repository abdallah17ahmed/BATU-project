Welcome to BATU

Borg Al-Arab Technological University (BATU) is a pioneering institution in Egypt, bridging academia and industry. Located in Borg Al-Arab city, BATU emphasizes practical education in fields like advanced manufacturing, IT, and renewable energy. With cutting-edge facilities and strong industry partnerships, BATU equips students with real-world skills and fosters innovation, driving technological advancement and community empowerment in the region.

Projects

Students of the Information Technology department at the university have completed over 82 projects in embedded systems. These projects address real-world challenges using advanced technologies. Their work highlights the importance of hands-on learning in developing technical expertise. By focusing on practical applications, the students enhanced their problem-solving and innovation skills. This achievement reflects their dedication to advancing the field of embedded systems.
