The First IT Exhibition

Under the patronage of:

  • Prof. Dr. Mohamed Morsi El-Gohary - President of Borg Al-Arab Technological University
  • Prof. Dr. Ibrahim El-Fahham - Dean of the Faculty of Industrial and Energy Technology
  • Dr. Osama El-Nahas - Head of the IT Program
  • Dr. Nihal El-Ozly - Event Coordinator

The IT Program at Borg Al-Arab Technological University proudly organized the First IT Exhibition. This event showcased 52 innovative projects in various IT fields, crafted by our talented students.

The exhibition aimed to foster creativity, encourage technological advancements, and provide students with a platform to present their work to industry professionals and peers.

Visitors explored cutting-edge projects in artificial intelligence, software development, network security, and much more. The event served as a bridge between academia and industry, enabling collaboration and idea exchange.
