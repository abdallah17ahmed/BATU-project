Video instructions

This page outlines the essential video recording guidelines for the project. It specifies content requirements, recording quality, duration limits, and rules to ensure clarity and professionalism.

  • Project Video:
    • The video must include all parts of the project.
    • The video should show the team actively working on the project.
    • All recording must be done in landscape mode (horizontal view).
    • The video must be recorded in high quality.
    • The total duration of the video should not exceed 2 minutes.
    • The video must not contain any effects, sounds, or additional edits.
