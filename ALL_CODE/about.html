Borg El Arab Technological University is a public Egyptian institution established under Law No. 72 of 2019, which governs the creation of technological universities in Egypt. These universities are educational entities focused on integrating education with practical training across various disciplines to meet the demands of the labor market. They also aim to develop and enhance students' skills, preparing them to excel in their respective fields.
