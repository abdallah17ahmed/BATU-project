The Second IT Exhibition

Under the patronage of:

  • Prof. Dr. Mohamed Morsi El-Gohary - President of Borg Al-Arab Technological University
  • Prof. Dr. Ibrahim El-Fahham - Dean of the Faculty of Industrial and Energy Technology
  • Dr. Osama El-Nahas - Head of the IT Program

The second IT exhibition was organized by Borg Al-Arab Technological University under the patronage of Prof. Dr. Ayman Ashour, Minister of Higher Education and Scientific Research. The exhibition was inaugurated by Prof. Dr. Mohamed Morsi El-Gohary, President of the University, and Prof. Dr. Ahmed El-Sabbagh, Advisor to the Minister of Higher Education for Technical Education.

The exhibition aimed to showcase innovative projects in the field of information technology, including artificial intelligence, big data, Internet of Things, and cybersecurity. The event provided a platform for students to present their projects to industry professionals, fostering collaboration and the exchange of ideas.

In his speech during the opening of the exhibition, Dr. Mohamed Morsi El-Gohary, President of the University, expressed his gratitude to the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research for its support and emphasized the vital role of information technology in the development and progress of society.

Dr. El-Gohary also highlighted the University's efforts to enhance students' skills in the field of IT through distinguished academic programs and strong partnerships with the private sector. He acknowledged the role of the Faculty of Industrial and Energy Technology in fostering a culture of innovation among students.

The University also announced the launch of the "Children's Technological University" initiative in Borg Al-Arab, aimed at educating and training young students and children in the latest technology fields.

In his remarks, Dr. Ahmed El-Sabbagh, Advisor to the Minister of Higher Education, emphasized the Ministry's support for technological universities and its commitment to producing qualified graduates who meet the demands of the job market.

The exhibition featured several booths showcasing the projects of the students of Borg Al-Arab Technological University. It also included discussions on the future of information technology, with experts from various fields sharing their insights.

The event also witnessed the opening of the "Internet of Things" laboratory, which was established through the efforts of faculty members and students of the IT program.

Prof. Dr. Ibrahim El-Fahham, Dean of the Faculty of Industrial and Energy Technology, stated that the exhibition was a wonderful opportunity for students to present their innovative projects and gain experience by interacting with experts in the field.

Dr. Osama El-Nahas, Head of the IT Program, expressed pride in the achievements of the students and affirmed that they would be future leaders in the IT sector.

At the conclusion of the event, Dr. Mohamed Morsi El-Gohary encouraged students to continue developing their skills and capabilities, highlighting that they represent the future of Egypt.
